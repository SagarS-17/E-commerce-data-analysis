# E-commerce-data-analysis

## Project Purpose:

To analyse sales data of a particular store.

## Goal:

To adjust pricing based on demand of products and analyze timeline to understand when sales happened.

## Data cleaning steps involved:

1.Dropping duplicate rows.

2.Removing white spaces from column names.

3.Cleaning and converting date values to appropriate format.

4.Cleaning and standardising product category column.

5.Cleaning quantity and price columns, dropping null values and converting to numerical values.

6.Cleaning total column and filling empty columns with product of price and quantity

## Business Impact:

Analysing historical sales trends helps predict future demands. This allows you to stock enough high-demand items to avoid lost revenue 
while reducing overstocking.

Analyzing the timeline of sales helps you with implementing discounts during low sales periods, which inturn could increase the sales.

## Results: 

Ultimately, the main focus of this project is prioritized roadmap of fixes.

According to the data, january 2025 saw the most sales with about $56,818. Home product category is most sold by total amount and blenders are the most sold product.

Shoes are the product most sold product by quantity.

Clothing has the most number of cancelled orders, this could be due to wrong size and fitting.

Headphones are the least sold product by both price and quantity.

Based on the data, more home product and fashion products should be easily available to the customers.
